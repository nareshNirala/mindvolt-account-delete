MindVolt

A brain-training and cognitive games app by MindRiseApps.

About MindVolt

MindVolt is a brain-training app designed to help users practice memory, focus, logic, and speed through short cognitive games, progress tracking, achievements, streaks, and leaderboard features.

Account and Data Deletion

Users can request deletion of their MindVolt account and associated data by contacting us at snky4u@gmail.com with the subject line Delete MindVolt Account.

If available in the app, users can also delete their account from: Settings > Delete Account.

Data Deleted

When an account is deleted, we delete the account profile, email-linked user ID, game progress, scores, XP, coins, streaks, achievements, and leaderboard data associated with that account.

Data Retained

Some data may be retained for a limited period if required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, dispute resolution, or backup recovery. Any retained data is kept only as long as necessary for these purposes.
